Mat Baker

Mat is passionate about music and rhythm. He has spent decades perfecting and evolving unique methods of music teaching. From guitar one to ones, to large kids' workshops at schools with drums & percussion at its core.

His understanding how music, sounds and rhythm interact with our body and mind has helped him to develop unique approaches, such as the workshop Rhythm into Stillness.


Bio


Mat is a solo artist and teacher (guitar, bouzouki, mandolin, ukulele, singing, drums & percussion).

He has performed live for over 35 years: solo, and alongside many international musicians. He's taught music for over 30 years, and has also recorded with a large range of international artists including Ladysmith Black Mambazo (South African acapella group).

Mat studied at the London Guitar Institute, as well as with various world renowned music teachers including Glen Velez and Reinhard Flatischler. He spent a number of years living in Spain, where he studied and soaked up the flamenco flavours.

He dedicates his career to both performing and teaching, sharing his passion for the joy of creating music.

Learn About Workshops


Rhythm into Stillness: join Mat for a rhythmical, meditative journey. A playful and challenging exploration into our body, mind and soul using drums, steps, claps, shakers and your voice.

  • Use different rhythmical patterns to support your growth in body awareness.
  • Starting energetically we will move towards a place of inner stillness.
  • No musical skills are necessary just an openness to explore.
