Mat Baker

Mat is a passionate, accomplished and sensitive musician. He is a guitarist, singer, multi instrumentalist and percussionist who naturally weaves rhythm and melody to create beautiful music for any event.

Mat is also an experienced music teacher and workshop facilitator. He has spent decades perfecting and evolving unique methods of music teaching to inspire his students of all ages and abilities.


Bio


Mat is a solo artist and teacher (guitar, bouzouki, ukulele, voice, drums & percussion).

He has performed live for over 35 years: solo, and alongside many international musicians. He's taught music for over 30 years, and has also recorded with a large range of international artists including Ladysmith Black Mambazo (South African acapella group).

Mat studied at the London Guitar Institute, as well as with various world renowned music teachers including Glen Velez (master frame drummer) and Reinhard Flatischler (Taketina). He has spent a number of years living in Spain, where he studied and soaked up the flamenco flavours.

He dedicates his career to both performing and teaching, sharing his passion for the joy of creating music.

Team Building Workshops

Drumming and rhythm are an incredible team building tool. They can be unifying, empowering and bonding. We can all join in a rhythm, irrespective of ability. The power of listening and building rhythm together is a powerful, primal experience.  Mat is an experienced and engaging workshop facilitator who brings lightness, fun and creativity to all his workshops.


School Workshops

Drum and Junk Percussion
Children love to drum! It is an engaging and educational experience that encourages focus, coordination and listening skills.

For younger children, drumming is a way to learn about numbers and develop concentration. For older children, it can help build confidence and creativity and support them to work together. All the time being playful and fun, whilst experiencing a connection to music in the most primal way. People have drummed all over the world for thousands of years.

Workshops are available for all ages from pre-school to year 12, including SEN.
Drums are provided.

Ukulele Workshops
The ukulele is an accessible, easy, fun instrument that can be a gateway into the magic of playing music. Learning it combines rhythm, melody, and chords. The ukulele is a beautiful instrument in its own right, whilst also being a natural introduction to the world of stringed instruments.

Workshops are suitable for all ages from 6 years, and can be for complete beginners or tailored for those with experience. Instruments provided depending on numbers.


Rhythm into Stillness

Join Mat for a rhythmical, meditative journey. A playful and challenging exploration into our body, mind and soul using drums, steps, claps, shakers and your voice.
  • Use different rhythmical patterns to support your growth in body awareness.
  • Starting energetically we will move towards a place of inner stillness.
  • No musical skills are necessary just an openness to explore.
